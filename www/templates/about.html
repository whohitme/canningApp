About this app

Welcome to the “Canning App”, which contains abbreviated checklists to guide you through the steps to successfully canning vegetables, fruits, meats and seafood. These checklists will remind those who have canned before of the steps in the canning process. While these checklists serve as guides, they are not inclusive of every step in food preparation, preservation and storage; home food preservers should always follow current, tested recipes and instructions to ensure their products are safe and good quality. Remember, if pressure canning, have gauge checked before use. For complete instructions and further details about preparing the food and the steps outlined, refer to the USDA Complete Guide to Home Canning or OSU Extension Service food preservation publications.
